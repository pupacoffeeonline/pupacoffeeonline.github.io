Our Story

It all started with a simple question: "Why should humans be the only ones who get coffee shop treats?" Pup A Coffee was created to bring the warmth, comfort, and excitement of visiting your favorite café into a completely dog-safe experience. Every recipe is handcrafted in small batches using carefully selected ingredients that are free from caffeine and designed specifically for dogs. We believe treat time should feel special—for both pets and their owners.
